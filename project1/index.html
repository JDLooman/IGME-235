Shaping the Lives of the Future

I am a Game Developer/Design student at RIT. Growing up, I loved to play video games and still remember many of them to this day. They shaped my childhood and now I want to create games to shape the childhood of new generations. When I'm not making games, I enjoy playing them.

Prison Escape:

Enter the world of the dark prison. You are currently an inmate trying to escape. Use the arrow keys to move and traverse multiple levels in search for the key. Find the key before getting caught or letting time run out.

Pokemon API:

Need to look up a Pokemon? This is just the thing for you. This Pokemon API allows you to search up any Pokemon and see thier stats, weaknesses, strengths and pictures. Using the DS model you can seamlessly switch between any of these to get exactly what you are looking for. Also, don't have a Pokemon in mind, use the random search to find a random Pokemon in its universe.
