Shaping the Lives of the Future

I am a Game Developer/Design student at RIT. Growing up, I loved to play video games and still remember many of them to this day. They shaped my childhood and now I want to create games to shape the childhood of new generations. When I'm not making games, I enjoy playing them.

Lights Out:

Lights Out is a third-person shoot-em-up game in the world of the Dresden Files. You are Harry Dresden, tasked to bring back light to Chicago to stop the ongoing infestation of zombies. Use your trusty sidearms and illusive magic to take down waves of zombies. Can Harry beat back the horde and bring light back to Chicago?

Karnissa's Castle:

Karnissa's Castle is a third-person puzzle game, in which two intrepid explorers have found themselves trapped in Karnissa's Castle, and their only way to escape is to work together to brew potions. Take control of both these explorers to solve riddles, brew potions, and move through the castle.

Prison Escape:

Enter the world of the dark prison. You are currently an inmate trying to escape. Use the arrow keys to move and traverse multiple levels in search for the key. Find the key before getting caught or letting time run out.
